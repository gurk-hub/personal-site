About Me

Up for a collaboration or just want to say hi? I'd love to hear from you.

I've wanted to make games ever since I started playing them. I realized quickly I'd need to learn a lot of different skills — and I did! I'm self-sufficient at everything needed to create a game, from making simple and efficient models to programming complex systems in Verse, such as world generation, custom menus, capture-point mechanics and a custom round system. I love making huge open-world games and boundary-pushing experiences.
